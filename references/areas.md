# Israeli Geographic Areas for Car Search

Valid area codes and regions for Yad2 vehicle searches.

## Primary Regions

### Center (מרכז)
**API Code**: `center`  
**Major Cities**: Tel Aviv, Ramat Gan, Petah Tikva, Rishon LeZion, Rehovot, Holon

### Tel Aviv (אזור תל אביב)
**API Code**: `tel aviv`  
**Coverage**: Tel Aviv metropolitan area, closest suburbs
**Notes**: Premium market, highest prices typically

### Jerusalem (ירושלים)
**API Code**: `jerusalem`  
**Coverage**: Jerusalem and surrounding areas

### Haifa (חיפה)
**API Code**: `haifa`  
**Coverage**: Haifa, Krayot, Carmel area

### North (צפון)  
**API Code**: `north`
**Major Areas**: Galilee, Golan Heights, Upper Galilee
**Cities**: Nazareth, Tiberias, Safed, Kiryat Shmona

### South (דרום)
**API Code**: `south`  
**Major Areas**: Negev, Eilat area
**Cities**: Beer Sheva, Ashdod, Ashkelon, Eilat

### Sharon (שרון)
**API Code**: `sharon`  
**Coverage**: Sharon region between Tel Aviv and Haifa  
**Major Cities**: Netanya, Ra'anana, Kfar Saba, Herzliya

### Judea and Samaria (יהודה ושומרון)
**API Code**: `judea and samaria`
**Coverage**: West Bank settlements

## Search Strategy

### Best Coverage Order
1. **Tel Aviv** - Largest market, most listings
2. **Center** - Broad central Israel coverage  
3. **Haifa** - Northern market
4. **Sharon** - Premium suburban market
5. **Jerusalem** - Unique market characteristics

### Area-Specific Notes

**Tel Aviv Area**:
- Highest prices due to premium location
- Most liquid market (fastest selling)
- Best selection for luxury/newer vehicles

**Center Region**:
- Good balance of price and selection
- Includes many family-oriented communities
- Broader geographic coverage than Tel Aviv

**Haifa Region**:
- Northern market, slightly different pricing
- Good for specific searches when Tel Aviv lacks results

**Sharon Region**:
- Affluent areas, premium vehicles common
- Ra'anana, Herzliya particularly upmarket

**Jerusalem**:  
- Unique market dynamics
- Religious community preferences may affect selection

**North/South**:
- Use when expanding search beyond central regions
- May have different pricing patterns

## Search Tips

- Start with `tel aviv` for maximum listings
- Use `center` for broader regional coverage
- Combine multiple areas for comprehensive market view
- Consider area-specific price premiums in valuation
